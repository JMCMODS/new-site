Inventory System

The inventory system serves as the foundation of a richer, more immersive roleplay experience. It allows players to seamlessly buy, collect, trade, and sell a wide variety of items across a dynamic range of locations — from bustling shops and corner stores to quirky hotdog stands and vibrant fruit stalls tucked throughout the city. Every item carries purpose: players can eat to satisfy hunger, drink to stay hydrated, smoke to relax, or use medical supplies to recover health. For those looking to take things further, the system even supports effects like getting high or drunk, influencing behavior and interactions in realistic, often unpredictable ways. With these features, every in-game choice feels impactful, and every encounter adds new layers of depth to your story.

Vehicle Persistence System

The vehicle saving system brings persistence and realism to your gameplay. Whether you park outside your house or leave your ride hidden in an alley, the system remembers your vehicle’s exact position and condition across sessions — ensuring it stays right where you left it, even after logging out or restarting.

In addition to this core functionality, the system includes seamless door locking and unlocking controls, giving you full ownership and security over your vehicle at all times. It's designed to be lightweight, intuitive, and fully integrated with the rest of the world, making it incredibly easy to use even for new players. Whether you're storing your personal car, fleeing a scene, or just roleplaying ownership, this system enhances immersion without adding unnecessary complexity.

Police System

The Police System adds a layer of realism to your driving experience. Officers can pull you over for offenses like using a mobile phone, speeding, or driving with a suspended license, as well as more serious violations such as driving under the influence or engine smoking. If you accumulate five violations, your driver’s license will be revoked, and your vehicle will be impounded. To retrieve it, you’ll need to pay a fine, and if you wait too long, your car could be permanently taken. During a traffic stop, a custom pull-over cutscene allows you to interact with the officer, where you can either accept the ticket and comply, or attempt to escape — but doing so increases the risk of harsher penalties. If caught with drugs or illegal money, the police will confiscate it. Your actions behind the wheel have real consequences, and how you handle the situation will determine whether you pay fines or lose your car for good.

Fuel Persistence System

The fuel persistence system introduces a new layer of realism and strategy to vehicle management. Only fuel-dependent vehicles now consume gas, while electric and non-fuel vehicles remain unaffected, offering players more variety and tactical choices in transportation. Scattered across the map, newly added gas stations bring life to the streets and serve as critical refueling points. With dynamically balanced and realistic fuel prices, players must plan their routes and manage their in-game finances carefully. Whether you're on a long road trip or escaping a high-stakes chase, keeping an eye on your fuel level is now more important than ever — adding tension, immersion, and authenticity to every journey.

Ped Creation System

The Character Creation System brings a fully customizable, GTA Online-style experience to life, giving players the tools to craft their unique identity from the ground up. Build your MP ped with precision — from facial features and hairstyles to clothing and accessories — and step into the world with a look that’s entirely your own. Newly opened clothing stores and barber shops across the city offer endless options for personal style, while a functional money and banking system gives your character true financial independence. Working ATMs make it easy to manage your cash flow, whether you're shopping, saving, or just trying to stay off the radar. You can also save and load multiple outfits, allowing you to switch looks on the fly for different occasions. This system isn’t just about appearance — it’s about creating a character that feels alive and grounded in a living, breathing world.

Car Control System

The Car Control System gives players full command over their vehicles, bringing depth, realism, and immersion to every drive. With intuitive controls, you can open and close doors, roll windows up or down, pop the hood, or access the trunk — perfect for roleplay scenarios and vehicle inspections. Buckle up with a working seatbelt system that enhances safety and immersion, especially during high-speed pursuits or collisions. Players can start or shut off their engine manually, activate cruise control for long drives, and rely on automatic turn signals that respond to steering direction. The custom speedometer UI offers a sleek, modern interface that displays speed, fuel, gear, and more at a glance, ensuring you're always in control. Whether you're cruising the city streets or preparing for a tactical getaway, this system makes every moment behind the wheel feel responsive, realistic, and alive.

The Black Market

The Black Market is your go-to source for all things illegal, offering a hidden world of underground deals for those willing to step outside the law. Whether you're looking for high-powered firearms, rare weapons, or ammunition, the Black Market has everything you need to arm yourself for any situation. Operating in the shadows, this secretive network allows you to purchase guns and ammo that can't be found in regular stores, offering everything from handguns to assault rifles — all at a price. But be cautious: these transactions are off the grid, and the wrong move could bring unwanted attention from both law enforcement and rival criminals. It's a world of risk and reward, where trust is scarce, and deals are made under the cover of darkness. Navigate carefully, because in the Black Market, anything goes.

Survival Elements

In the unforgiving and hostile world of GTA5, survival is more than just a game — it's a constant struggle. As hunger and dehydration can quickly turn into life-threatening issues. You’ll need to manage your hunger and hydration levels meticulously to ensure you don't suffer from debilitating effects that can drastically impact your health, stamina, and even your ability to think clearly. A few missteps can lead to dizziness, weakness, or worse, leaving you vulnerable to the dangers around you.

Throughout the sprawling city, there are countless places to keep your belly full and your thirst quenched. Whether you're picking up a meal from a bustling grocery store, grabbing a quick snack at a hot dog stand, or purchasing refreshing drinks from a local fruit vendor, you'll need to keep an eye out for these essential resources. Each location offers a unique opportunity to stock up, and staying prepared is key.

DMV Center

The DMV system in GTA5 adds a layer of realism and immersion, allowing players to obtain their driving licenses through both theory and practical tests. Whether you're looking to drive a car, ride a bike, or operate a truck, you'll need to pass specific tests that mirror the challenges real drivers face. The custom-made theory tests cover essential topics like traffic laws, road signs, and safe driving practices, ensuring you're well-prepared for your practical exam.

The practical driving tests are just as rigorous. You'll be required to demonstrate your skills on the road, proving that you can handle your vehicle under various conditions. Whether it's navigating through busy streets in a car, handling tight corners on a bike, or managing the size and weight of a truck, each test is designed to challenge your ability to operate different types of vehicles safely..

Built-In Jobs / Activities

PSRP offers a wide variety of jobs and activities, each with unique rewards and challenges. For those who enjoy a more laid-back lifestyle, fishing allows you to catch and sell different types of fish, while hunting lets you gather pelts and meat from animals. These activities are perfect for making money while exploring the world at your own pace. If you prefer a riskier approach, drug dealing gives you the opportunity to work the underground market, but be ready for the dangers and consequences that come with it.

For those looking for more traditional work, Bolt (Uber) lets you earn money by picking up passengers and driving them around the city, while truck driving involves transporting goods across the map, testing your navigation skills and stamina. Another option is selling scrap, where you collect discarded materials and sell them for cash, turning junk into profit. Each of these jobs provides a different way to earn money and offers a fresh experience, adding depth to the world of GTA5.
